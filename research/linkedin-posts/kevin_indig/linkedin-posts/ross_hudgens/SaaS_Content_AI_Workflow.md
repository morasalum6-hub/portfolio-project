## Post 1 – How Siege Media Uses AI in Content Production

**Key idea:** AI is now embedded in every stage of content production at Siege Media — research, outlining, drafting, and optimization. But the human layer is where quality is created.

**Takeaway for playbook:** AI handles the volume, humans handle the judgment. The ratio matters — too much AI without editing produces content that ranks nowhere.

---

## Post 2 – The ROI of Content for SaaS

**Key idea:** Content marketing for SaaS has a long payback period but compounds over time. AI accelerates the timeline by reducing production costs, but strategy still determines ROI.

**Takeaway for playbook:** AI content without a distribution and linking strategy is wasted effort. Treat AI as a production accelerator, not a strategy replacement.

---

## Post 3 – Quality Signals in the AI Content Era

**Key idea:** Google is getting better at detecting low-quality AI content. The sites that win are those that add original research, real examples, and expert quotes that AI can't fabricate.

**Takeaway for playbook:** Every AI-generated article needs at least one element that AI couldn't have produced — a case study, a data point, an expert quote.
