## Post 1 – AI Content at Scale

**Key idea:** AI makes content production faster but not better by default. The bottleneck has shifted from writing to editing and quality control.

**Takeaway for playbook:** Build a review layer into every AI content workflow. Speed without quality destroys rankings.

---

## Post 2 – SEO for SaaS in the AI Era

**Key idea:** SaaS companies that win at SEO in 2025 are those that combine product expertise with AI-assisted content production. Generic content gets ignored by both Google and LLMs.

**Takeaway for playbook:** The best AI SEO content comes from subject matter experts who use AI to scale their knowledge, not from AI writing about things it doesn't know.

---

## Post 3 – What Actually Moves the Needle

**Key idea:** After years at Shopify and G2, the pattern is clear: topic authority beats individual keyword targeting. Build a content cluster around one core topic before expanding.

**Takeaway for playbook:** Use AI to fill out content clusters systematically — not to chase random keywords.
