# AI Impact on SEO – Aleyda Solis (Orainti)

Author: Aleyda Solis – International SEO Consultant, Orainti
Platform: LinkedIn + SEOFOMO Newsletter

## Key Points

- AI Overviews and LLM-based search are fundamentally changing which content gets visibility
- SEO is not dead — but the type of content that ranks is shifting toward expertise and originality
- AI tools should be used to scale research and ideation, not to replace human expertise and perspective
- The biggest risk with AI content is producing generic, undifferentiated material that adds no value
- International SEO + AI requires extra care: AI often generates inaccurate or culturally wrong content for non-English markets
- Entity-based SEO is more important than ever — who you are matters as much as what you publish
- EEAT (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) is the framework Google uses to evaluate AI-assisted content
- Monitoring AI Overview appearances for your target keywords is now a core SEO task
