# AI SEO Content Workflows for Startups – Nate Matherson (Positional)
Video/Podcast: https://www.linkedin.com/in/natematherson/
Author: Nate Matherson – Co-founder, Positional
Platform: Optimize Podcast + LinkedIn

## Key Points

- Most startups waste time on SEO content that will never rank — topic selection is the highest-leverage decision
- AI tools are most useful for research, outlining, and first drafts — human editing is non-negotiable
- Programmatic SEO at scale requires a content quality threshold or Google will ignore the entire site
- The best AI SEO workflow: keyword research → search intent analysis → AI draft → human expert review → publish
- Internal linking is massively underused by startups and dramatically affects which pages rank
- Content velocity matters but content quality determines whether velocity pays off
- Measuring content performance requires patience — most SEO content takes 3-6 months to show results
- Positional was built because existing tools didn't connect content production to actual ranking outcomes

## Why This Source Matters
Nate Matherson built an SEO tool specifically for startups and documents real workflows used by growth teams. His podcast covers actual case studies, not theory — making it one of the highest-signal sources on AI content production for B2B SaaS.
