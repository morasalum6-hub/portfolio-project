# He Tested 750 SEO Factors – Kyle Roof
Video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CdfYQnE81sw
Author: Kyle Roof – PageOptimizer Pro
Date: July 2025

## Key Points

- Kyle Roof holds an SEO patent and has tested over 750 ranking factors in controlled experiments
- Most of what the SEO industry obsesses over turned out to be irrelevant or overstated
- Data-driven testing is the only reliable way to know what Google actually rewards
- AI-generated content can rank well when it covers the right entities and semantic signals
- On-page optimization based on real data beats assumptions every time
- The shift to AI search (LLMs, SGE) changes how content needs to be structured
- Entity clarity and structured data matter more than backlinks for AI-driven search results
- Even unreadable or poorly designed content can rank if it has the right signals

## Why This Source Matters
Kyle Roof is one of the few SEOs who runs scientific, controlled experiments instead of theorizing. His findings directly inform how to use AI tools for content production without wasting effort on factors that don't move the needle.
