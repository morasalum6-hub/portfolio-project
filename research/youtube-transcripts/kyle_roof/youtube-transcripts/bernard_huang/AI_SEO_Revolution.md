# AI-Driven SEO Revolution – Bernard Huang (Clearscope)
Video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f84ovVChEh4
Author: Bernard Huang – Co-founder, Clearscope
Date: November 2025

## Key Points

- The problem is no longer just ranking in blue links — it's influencing the answer AI gives to users
- Two developments rewired search: LLMs made content generation trivial, and AI search changed user behavior
- Content optimization now means covering all relevant entities, not just hitting keywords
- Brands that prove expertise through video and original research will outperform generic AI content
- AI content has dropped the cost of mediocre content to near zero — quality and authority matter more than ever
- Clearscope gives insights into what entities and related topics need to be covered for comprehensive content
- Evergreen content is not actually evergreen — it needs continuous monitoring and refreshing
- Content decay is a real metric: pages that peaked and are now losing traffic need active intervention

## Why This Source Matters
Bernard Huang built one of the leading AI-powered SEO tools (Clearscope) and shares the most practical frameworks for using AI in content production at scale. His insights come from real data across thousands of enterprise content teams.
