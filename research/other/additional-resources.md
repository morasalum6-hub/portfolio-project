## Key Concepts Identified Across Sources

- **Entity-based SEO** – Covering related topics and concepts, not just target keywords
- **EEAT** – Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness
- **Content decay** – Pages losing traffic over time that need refreshing
- **Programmatic SEO** – Using templates and data to produce content at scale
- **AI Overviews / SGE** – Google's AI-generated search results that change what ranks
- **AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)** – Optimizing for AI-generated answers, not just blue links
